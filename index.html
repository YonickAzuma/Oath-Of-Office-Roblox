About this game

Oath of Office: Political Simulator is a political role-playing game created by Jack Jennings and developed by Chesapeake Studios. The game was released on September 1, 2024, and allows players to take on important positions within the United States government, ranging from the President and cabinet members to the media and security.

The Legislative Branch creates laws and oversees the actions of the Executive Branch. It consists of the Senate

The Senate, as part of the United States Congress, is a legislative body with 8 state (two per state) designed to provide equal representation for all states regardless of population size. It shares lawmaking responsibilities with the House of Representatives, but with distinct duties like confirming presidential appointments, ratifying treaties, conducting impeachment trials, and acting as a deliberative body that ensures laws are carefully scrutinized and national decisions are broadly supported.

The House of Representatives is one of the two chambers of the United States Congress, with the Senate being the other. It is responsible for initiating legislation, particularly bills related to revenue and spending, as well as representing the population of each state based on its population size. Each stateâ€™s representation is determined by its population, ensuring that more populous states have a greater voice in the legislative process.
